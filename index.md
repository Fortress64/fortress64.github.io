Fortress64 is a small team of Linux and GNU enthusiasts building privacy-respecting Android ROM and recovery releases for a select range of devices. Our builds are designed to reduce dependence on proprietary Google services while remaining practical for everyday use.

We maintain custom ROM builds based on CrDroid, LineageOS, and other AOSP-derived distributions — extended with a curated set of open-source apps, OTA update support, MicroG integration, and tailored security patches.

## What We Offer

**Custom ROMs** — Privacy-hardened Android builds for supported devices, with OTA updates, MicroG, and a configurable app selection system.

**Custom Recoveries** — TWRP, OrangeFox, and Pitch Black Recovery Project builds for each supported device, bundled with the latest Magisk release.

**Android Live Recovery USB** — A compact, bootable toolkit for safely de-bricking and managing Android devices without modifying the host system. [Download](/downloads/android-live-recovery-usb/)

**Research** — Independent documentation of manufacturer bootloader restrictions, hardware risks, and community findings. [Read more](/research/vendor-restrictions/)

**Installation Manuals** — Step-by-step flashing guides for Xiaomi and Samsung devices, covering unlocking, flashing, and recovery. [Xiaomi](/manuals/xiaomi-installation/) · [Samsung](/manuals/samsung-installation/)

## Our Approach

We believe your device should work for you, not for advertisers. Our builds ship without Google Play Services by default, using [MicroG](https://microg.org/) as a drop-in replacement that preserves app compatibility while eliminating the privacy implications of the full Google stack.

All included apps are open-source, removable, and selected for their respect of user privacy. The full app list and configuration system are documented on each device's release page.
